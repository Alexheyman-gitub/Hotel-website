About Hotel BT

The Boston Suites are aptly named due to their wonderful views beyond the hotel's fountain to the greenery. The generous proportions of these suites provide separate spacious and airy living and sleeping areas complete with walk-in closets and a marble bathroom with double sinks. Situated on the corners of the hotel, Beverly Suites have unparalleled views from the windows as well as generous balconies where guests can choose to dine or take in the sunshine. The sitting room is elegantly styled and flooded with natural light.
